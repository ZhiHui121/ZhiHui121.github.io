Principals of fMRI and experiments related to imagination

Author: Zhi Hui Date: December 11, 2024  21:04:55 Category: Tech

fMRI is just one of many methods developed to study the relationship between human brain activity and behavior. To advance our understanding in this area, we have been focusing on two key directions:

  1. Obtaining precise biological measures from the human brain.
  2. Modeling human behaviors in non-human animals.

Several methods have been developed to achieve these goals, including:

  1. fMRI (functional Magnetic Resonance Imaging)
  2. EEG (Electroencephalography)
  3. PTE (Positron Emission Tomography)
  4. NIRS (Near-Infrared Spectroscopy)

The advantages of fMRI are:

  1. Non-invasive
  2. High temporal resolution: It can measure brain activity within seconds.
  3. Whole-brain coverage: It captures activity from the entire brain and provides insights into interactions between different brain areas.
